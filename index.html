About

The pivot has a paper trail.

I spent a decade as a special education case manager in Chicago — coordinating IEPs, navigating compliance timelines, and watching skilled professionals spend half their day on documentation instead of students. That friction became my obsession.

Before teaching, I worked in tech sales serving law firms and life sciences companies, and in PR and field operations at a private utility. I've seen how institutions resist change — and how the right tool, introduced the right way, actually sticks.

Today I build AI-powered workflows and automation systems for education teams and legal professionals — the exact niches where I know the pain points from the inside.
